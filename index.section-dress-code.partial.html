Dress Code.

Honestly, we couldn't decide with a dress code for a long time, because all our friends are creative guys, and we did not want to tell you what to wear at our event. Nevertheless, the wedding will be in a rustic style, so please pay attention to our recommendations below.

Ladies

We ask you to choose plain, light dresses without rhinestones or spangles. The space will be decorated in pastel colors, unseparated with red and green. Given that the ceremony will take place on the roof, we recommend that you do not wear floor-length gowns.

Gentlemen

Open collar, please, instead of a classic tie. you can wear a bow tie if you wish. In fact, we do not have specific recommendations for men. You can wear a light two-piece suit or just be in a white shirt and trousers. Please avoid dark colors and vibrant prints.
